About Me

I'm a 25 year-old graduate with a passion for all things digital and visual. I graduated with a degree in History from the University of Birmingham in 2017 during which I spent a year abroad at the University of Malta.

I currently work in the heritage sector, having previously worked for Historic Royal Palaces and the Commonwealth War Graves Commission. I'm looking towards combining my interests in coding and heritage through the realm of digital heritage.

For this reason I decided to return to the University of Birmingham for the Full Stack Web Development course. I am currently developing a strong portfolio of web projects, some of which have been included on my portfolio page!
